Research

I'm a Ph.D. student at UF studying post-transcriptional regulation of acid stress survival systems in E. coli and the implications this may have in the microbiome. My goals are to use computational methods and approaches wet-lab approaches to be able to explore regulation and interactions in greater depth. I'm planning to graduate in the Spring of 2022 and am currently interested in post-doc and industry positions.
